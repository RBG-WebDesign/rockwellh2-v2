The Hardware Stack

Modular Infrastructure for Localized Production.

Rockwell H2 Systems replaces the brittle hydrogen supply chain with site-integrated hardware. Our modular "Pods" allow industrial users to generate fuel exactly where it is consumed, eliminating 80% of logistics-related energy waste.

  • H2-POD: Production

    Advanced PEM electrolysis optimized for fluctuating renewable input.

  • S-POD: Storage

    Cascading high-pressure storage with triple-redundant safety venting.

  • D-POD: Dispensing

    Heavy-duty 350/700 bar dispensing capable of filling a Class-8 truck in < 15 mins.
